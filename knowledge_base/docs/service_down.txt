RUNBOOK: Internal Service Down or Slow (Jenkins, Jira, Confluence)
Common Causes:
1. Scheduled maintenance window
2. Service pod crashed
3. High traffic exhausting connections
Resolution Steps:
1. Check service health status
2. Check internal status page for active incidents
3. If service is down, escalate immediately to L2-Platform
4. If service is up but user still affected, ask them to clear cache
